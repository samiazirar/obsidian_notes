## Working Rules

- Use daily notes for fast capture first.
- Move anything with longer shelf life into a project, literature, supervision, or idea note.
- Keep one note per project, paper, thesis, or idea unless there is a clear reason to split.
- Prefer plain Markdown headings and YAML properties so the vault stays machine-readable.
